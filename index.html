About Us

Empowering Students Through Global Education

Shali Education Resources Limited is a trusted educational travel and study abroad consultancy dedicated to helping students achieve their international education goals. We provide professional guidance and personalized support for students seeking quality education opportunities abroad.

Our services include study visa assistance, university and college application support, admission processing, travel arrangements, flight reservations, accommodation guidance, and pre-departure support. We work closely with students and parents to simplify the entire process, ensuring a smooth transition from application to arrival at their destination.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Processing times vary by country. Typically, study visa processing takes between 2-8 weeks depending on the destination country and time of year. We recommend starting the process at least 3-4 months before your intended departure date to allow ample time for document preparation and processing.

Requirements vary by country and institution, but typically include: a valid passport, academic transcripts, English proficiency test results (IELTS/TOEFL), statement of purpose, reference letters, proof of funds, and medical clearance. Our consultants will guide you through the specific requirements for your chosen destination.

Yes! We help students identify and apply for scholarships, grants, and bursaries available at their chosen institutions. We also provide guidance on government-sponsored scholarship programs and external funding opportunities that can significantly reduce your education costs.

Most countries allow international students to work part-time during their studies. For example, the UK allows up to 20 hours per week, Canada allows 20 hours per week off-campus, and Australia permits 48 hours per fortnight. We provide detailed guidance on work regulations for your specific destination.

While our visa success rate is 98%, we also prepare for every scenario. If a visa is rejected, we analyze the reasons, address the deficiencies in your application, and help you reapply with a stronger case. In some cases, we may suggest alternative destinations or institutions that better match your profile.
